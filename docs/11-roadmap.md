## Phase 17 Direction

Phase 17 establishes KRAVIA Cloud Platform as shared infrastructure for VaanForge and future KRAVIA products. Future phases should build on KCP instead of duplicating identity, gateway, event, storage, secrets, configuration, messaging, AI runtime, build, deploy, monitoring, observability, billing, or console services.
