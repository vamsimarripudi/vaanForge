# Future Phases

Future requirements must be documented here before implementation.

When a new requirement appears:

- Document it.
- Prioritize it.
- Add it to future phases.
- Do not break previous architecture.

The architecture must support growth for at least 5-10 years.

Initial candidates:

- Full cookie authentication and account creation.
- Workspace invite flow.
- Finance revenue and expense CRUD.
- P&L, GST, and cash-flow generators with formulas.
- HR hiring and interview workflows.
- Legal document templates with disclaimers.
- Support live chat and SLA workflows.
- Report export generation.
- Production Razorpay billing integration, approved commercial prices, and webhook reconciliation.
- Production-grade audit storage.
