# Business Reports

Business reports provide exportable snapshots for leadership, finance, support, marketplace, security, deployment, AI usage, and developer operations.

## Report Types

- Executive
- Finance
- Billing
- Support
- Marketplace
- Security
- Deployment
- AI Usage
- Developer

## Formats

- PDF-ready HTML
- CSV
- Excel-ready CSV

## APIs

- `GET /api/v1/admin/business/reports`
- `POST /api/v1/admin/business/reports`

Each generated report is stored with report type, format, status, content, generating user, and timestamp.
