Additional Required Public / Legal / Support Pages

Even though VaanForge should open directly into the product workspace, the product still needs essential public, legal, trust, and support pages. These pages must be clean, unique, enterprise-grade, and consistent with the VaanForge product UI.

Important:
Do not make these pages feel like generic marketing pages.
Do not use parent company branding.
Use only “VaanForge”.
Keep the design simple, premium, readable, and product-focused.

Page Design Requirements:
- All pages must share the same VaanForge design system
- Product name only: VaanForge
- No parent company name
- No old suite references
- No generic stock layout
- No fake data
- No decorative clutter
- Mobile-first
- Responsive
- Strong typography
- Clear page purpose
- Useful empty/loading/error states where applicable
- Top nav should remain consistent
- Sidebar should not appear unless user is inside the app workspace
- Footer should be minimal and useful
